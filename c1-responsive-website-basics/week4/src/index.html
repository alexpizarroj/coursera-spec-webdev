Pia Alonzo Wurtzbach

Pia Alonzo Wurtzbach (born September 24, 1989) is a Filipino actress, TV Host, model and beauty queen who was crowned Miss Universe 2015 on December 20, 2015 after becoming Miss Universe Philippines 2015 at the Binibining Pilipinas 2015 pageant.

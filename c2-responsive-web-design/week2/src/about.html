A place to plan

If you've ever struggled with time managment or getting things done, this is the right place for you: Organize yourself and your team, create tasks, set deadlines and assign people to work on each issue.

A place to keep track of your progress

Everyone can watch each other's progress by just heading to the corresponding task page. Is any of you stuck on a task? Ask for help, write a private message or leave a comment on someone's task.
